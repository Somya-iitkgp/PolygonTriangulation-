Polygon Triangulation


The Ear Clipping algorithm used in this application can tesselate concave polygons.
Draw your own polygon and see in depth working of Ear clipping algorithm.




Note-
  • Draw the points in anticlockwise order.
  • Green Color point indicates vertex has been clipped.
  • Yellow color point indicates this triangle is being clipped.
  • Blue color point indicates the vertices of current polygon.
